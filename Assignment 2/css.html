Introduction

Pokhara is a beautiful city in Nepal.It is very famous for the tourism. The place decorated by natural beauty seems a heaven of life.People from all over the world come to visit and enjoy the culture. Not only the the geographically rich also the cultural diversity. Very small area but big amount of qualities that can be found there.Due to the high population density and the frequency of natural disasters, western Nepal is considered one of the most disaster-prone regions in the world.

Location

Pokhara is a metropolitan city in Nepal, which serves as the capital of Gandaki Province. It is the second most populous city of Nepal after Kathmandu, with 518,452 inhabitants living in 101,669 households in 2021. Pokhara is located in mid-western part of Nepal in Kaski district, and Province 5.It has moderate climate and famous in rainning.There always rains once in a day. People enjoy the climate with neither hot nor cold there. Domestic tourits and international tourists are increasing day by day.

Importance

It is the gateway to Annapurna trekking. It is mostly famous for trekking, boating, hiking, pony rides, paragliding and the stunning view of Annapurna Mountain range. So, it is the favourite and top priority place for most of the tourists. Once who visited Nepal has visited Pokhara.Pokhara is the home to numerous lakes. Fewa lake being the most famous includingBegnaslake, Rupa lake, Gude lake, Neurani lake and Maidi lake among many others.
